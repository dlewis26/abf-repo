ABOUT THE FISH HOUSE

The Fish House has been serving the Americaus community since 1939! We offer a variety of seafood including but not limited to fresh fish, shrimp, crab legs and oysters. We also serve wings and burgers. And don't be afraid to add bottled beer or wine to your order to enjoy with your meal!
